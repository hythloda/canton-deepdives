Introduction

The Canton Network Deep Dive series is a public meeting place for technical presentations about the canton-network open source projects. Its scope is all current and confirmed upcoming repositories under github.com/canton-network.

Each week has a single presentation from one or more contributors. Presentations are expected to run 30-45 minutes, followed by a general AMA session for the core contributors. Questions should be scoped to the canton-network OSS repositories and will be moderated each week.

Two weekly time slots are reserved for a globally distributed contributor network: 6am US ET and 10am US ET. The default is 10am ET, but speakers may request the 6am ET slot instead. All presentations will be recorded and made public.
